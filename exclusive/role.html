Requirements to Claim a Role

Basic Requirements

  • You must be a member of our Discord server.
  • Your Discord account must be at least 7 days old.
  • Your email must be verified.
  • Your account must not be flagged by Discord.

Activity Requirements

  • Content Creator Role: At least 3 published videos.
  • Designer / Editor Role: Minimum 2 samples.
  • Gaming Role: Active in one game community.

Server Requirements

  • No mute/ban/warning in last 7 days.
  • Readable username required.

How It Works

  1. Submit the form below.
  2. Your details are sent to our Discord webhook.
  3. Bot will verify and assign your role.
